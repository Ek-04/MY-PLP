I AM WANGARI

INTRO(Do you like poetry?🤭coz I doooo!!!🤓)

I am Wangari, a damsel techie! Riding on the wings of technology so rocky As I trudge in the tapestry of life’s murky, I hope that my discipline and strife will not leave me empty.
Hope and faith are so dear to me, and gift of friendship is my guiding bridge, As I trudge in the tapestry of life’s murky, I hope that the connections I’ve made will not be flaky
Dreams and goals are so near to my heart, I just pray that they will not leave me hurt As I trudge through the tapestry of life’s murky, I hope that the aspirations I have will label me lucky!
